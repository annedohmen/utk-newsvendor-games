Two examples of calculating the CF in a period follow below:

  1. Example 1:
    • selling price: 20 USD / unit
    • purchasing price: 7.5 USD / unit
    • inventory holding costs: 5 USD / unit
    • starting inventory 10 units
    • order quantity: 50 units
    • available inventory: 60 units [ = 10 units available inventory + 50 units order quantity]
    • demand: 20 units

    CF = 20 USD * 20 units [units sold = Minimum of demand and available inventory] - 7.5 USD * 50 units [order quantity] - 5 USD * 40 units [final inventory= available inventory - units sold]

      = -175 USD
  2. Example 2:
    • demand: 100 units
    • other constants from example 1 apply

    CF = 20 USD * 60 units [units sold = Minimum of demand and available inventory] - 7.5 USD * 50 units [order quantity] - 5 USD * 0 units [final inventory= 0]

      = 825 USD
